About The Project

Project Japan Definitive Edition is a complete Android GTA SA visual overhaul focused on realism, atmosphere, improved textures, modern lighting, and enhanced gameplay visuals.

The goal of the project is to deliver a next-generation mobile GTA San Andreas experience while keeping the game optimized for Android devices.
